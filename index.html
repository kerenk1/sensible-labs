What is Supernatural?

Supernatural is the first mobile game designed to teach and facilitate CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) practice for youth and adolescents.

Supernatural makes anxiety treatment an enjoyable experience that becomes easily maintainable.

Millennials and Gen Z are the most anxious generations and are less able to manage their anxiety compared to any other generation. They also find it very difficult to be persistent about CBT therapy.

Who is it for?
How do I know if it is for me?

It is suitable for anyone who feels stressed and anxious, whether diagnosed with anxiety or not.

At the beginning of the game, we evaluate your anxiety level and make sure it is low-moderate severe, so you'll be sure the game will be effective.
